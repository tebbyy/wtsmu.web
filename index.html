meet the game

When the sunshine melts us

Frosti, a zippy and gleeful snowman, faces an adventure in Villa Hornero to discover the brimming mystery of the power outage that left his beloved fridge out of service. Meanwhile, a suffocating heat wave, attributed to the local factory, is melting the town's spirits. Despite his reduced mobility due to his lack of legs, and even with the risk of melting under the blazing sun, the little Frosti manages to propel himself using centrifugal force to overcome the absurd tasks the townies give him, all to help restore the villa's cold shine. Will Frosti dare to brave the infernal heat, confront the factory pollution, and bring back the freshness to the town and his dearest fridge?
